Michelle Dee wins 'Best National Costume' award at Miss Universe 2023

By: GMA Integrated News

Michelle Dee is still winning!

        advertisement
        
        The Philippines’ Michelle Dee just earned another award from the recent Miss Universe pageant!
        
        On Friday evening, Sparkle GMA Artist Center shared the exciting news and congratulated Michelle.
        
        “With the most votes on the Choicely app, MMD has won the coveted 'Best National Costume' award at the Miss Universe 2023,” Sparkle wrote.
        
        During the National Costume competition, Michelle paid tribute to the Filipino weaving culture and her role as an Air Force Reservist.
